About Us

Kennels

Our stalls are 5X5 with elevated pet beds and blankets. Each stall is connected to an outdoor run that is 12 feet long, with a hanging Jolly ball for entertainment. Our kennel is temperature controlled, with a radio for relaxation. Our kennels are disinfected daily with Synbiont.

Furry Tails

Our kennel is 50 feet from our home. It can be viewed from a side window providing round the clock supervision in a quiet, rural environment. Our main priority is for your pet to be happy and comfortable while you are away.

Food and Toys

Pet parents are asked to bring your pets food with you so their diet is not changed. It is also ok to bring favorite toys or blankets. Your dog will also need to have a leash and collar. Dogs from the same family can stay with each other in the same stall, limit 2 dogs per stall.
